About Silver Hornets

Our Mission

Silver Hornets Team 21331 is dedicated to inspiring and preparing students for careers and further education in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) through competitive robotics. We believe in the power of teamwork, innovation, and perseverance.

What is FTC?

FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is a robotics competition for middle school students. Teams design, build, and program robots to compete in an alliance format against other teams. The competition combines the excitement of sport with the rigors of science and technology.

Our Values

Integrity

We compete with honor and respect for all teams and judges.

Innovation

We embrace creative solutions and continuous improvement.

Collaboration

We work together as one team, supporting each other's growth.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all we do, both on and off the field.

Team History

Silver Hornets Team 21331 is committed to advancing STEM education and creating a supportive environment where students can explore their passion for robotics and engineering. Our team has grown significantly, with members from diverse backgrounds bringing unique perspectives and skills to our projects.
